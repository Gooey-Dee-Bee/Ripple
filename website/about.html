About Us

Ripple is the best new way to share the music you love with people around you. You can drop a song for the other users in your area to see. If they like it, they can redrop it. The more people that redrop your song, the farther it will spread and the more points you will get.

Point System

Dropping a song costs you 10 points and redropping is 5 points. For every redrop of your song, you get points back. The more people that redrop your song, the more points you get. The first person to redrop your song will earn you 1 point and the second earns you 2 points, the third 3 points, and so on.

But I hate this song, can I downvote it?

No, unfortunately you cannot downvote any song because your music taste probably sucks. Everyone's music tastes are slightly different, so a song you hate is probably the best song in the world to Nickelback. To each his own, right?
