Welcome to Ripple, the home of the hottest songs on the internet. If you are looking for the songs that people in your area are listening to, then you came to the right place.

The people in your area have "dropped" some pretty sweet songs and you can too. All you have to do is search for a song or enter the SoundCloud URL into the search bar, and we will show you some sick songs that you can drop. We search all of the songs on SoundCloud to show you the best ones.

Each drop costs you 10 points, and you can redrop songs for 5 points. If other people redrop your songs, you earn points back. The first person to redrop your songs gets you 1 point, the second person gets you 2 points, the third 3 points, etc.
